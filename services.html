Our Dental Services

Complete care for your whole family

Paediatric Dentistry

We encourage routine dental visits for a healthy experience rather than only when in pain. We invest time and resources to make children comfortable and happy during visits. Regular 6-month checkups help detect cavities early, allowing less traumatic and more cost-effective treatment options.

Preventive Dentistry

We conduct full oral examinations for all new patients to detect abnormalities. Our treatment plans are tailored to address health issues. Regular checkups and oral health education are part of our commitment to long-term dental wellness.

Restorative Dentistry

We rebuild decayed or broken teeth using durable restorative materials. Our focus is preserving teeth with minimal extraction. We use fluoride-leaching, cavity-adaptable composite fillings for both children and adults.

Endodontic Treatments

Root canal treatment removes dead pulp tissue, seals the roots, and prevents infection. We use rapid radiographic imaging and advanced endo-motor systems to ensure complete sealing and long-lasting treatment.

Periodontic Treatments

We treat gum, ligament, and jawbone diseases like gingivitis and periodontal disease. Early intervention prevents tooth loss and supports the success of other dental treatments.

Prosthetic Treatments

Tooth loss impacts chewing, speaking, and aesthetics. We restore function using porcelain crowns, chrome/acrylic dentures, and effective, efficient techniques.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Our cosmetic services like veneers and whitening improve self-confidence. We use high-tech materials and offer in-chair or at-home whitening, with laser whitening also available.
