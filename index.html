Madaraka Dental

Was opened on 15th March 2021 and still in operation. The initial staff structure consisted of one dentist, one dental assistant and a receptionist. During the course of the last few months in operation, the staff base has increased to include one more dental practitioner and a support staff. To date, Madaraka Dental Centre has one fully functioning operational dental unit with a dental specialist available for specialized dental care. The Clinic offers affordable dental services to our different classes of clients hence witnessing an increase in the number of patients visiting.
