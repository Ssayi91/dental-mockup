Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure availability.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes, please inform us at least 24 hours in advance to cancel or reschedule.

Do you accept walk-ins?

We accept walk-ins subject to availability. However, appointments take priority.

What dental services do you offer?

We offer general dentistry, cosmetic procedures (such as whitening and veneers), root canal therapy, extractions, pediatric dentistry, and orthodontics.

Is dental cleaning painful?

Most cleanings are not painful. You may feel some discomfort if you have gum sensitivity, but we make every effort to keep you comfortable.

How often should I visit the dentist?

It’s recommended to visit the dentist every 6 months for a routine checkup and cleaning, even if you’re not experiencing any pain.

Do you offer teeth whitening?

Yes, we provide professional teeth whitening that is safe, fast, and effective.

Do you treat children?

Absolutely! We offer gentle and friendly pediatric dental care tailored for children of all ages.

Do you accept SHA and if so which scheme? and any other insurance?

We accept a range of insurance providers. Please contact us to confirm if your insurance is supported.
