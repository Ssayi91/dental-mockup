Our Story

Madaraka Dental Centre Ltd is registered under Companies Registration Acts as Madaraka Dental centre Ltd. It is incorporated in Kenya and fully Kenyan owned.

Madaraka Dental Centre was opened on 15th March 2021 and still in operation. The initial staff structure consisted of one dentist, one dental assistant and a receptionist. During the course of the last few months in operation, the staff base has increased to include one more dental practitioner and a support staff. To date, Madaraka Dental Centre has one fully functioning operational dental unit with a dental specialist available for specialized dental care. The Clinic offers affordable dental services to our different classes of clients hence witnessing an increase in the number of patients visiting.

Our Equipment

Fully Functioning Operational Dental Unit

A well maintained and serviced dental unit improves the ability of the dental practitioner to provide a high level of dental care for our patients. Infection control is improved by including a suction, cuspidor and an ultrasonic scaler into the dental chair. Through manipulation, the dentist can easily adjust the sitting position of the patient for ease of treatment and visualization

Intra-Oral Camera

The Intra-Oral Camera allows the dentist to examine patient’s teeth from close range and to illustrate to the patient the things they would like to see in their oral cavities. They assist patients to visualize their oral cavities. These intraoral photos are saved in patient files in our database and can be shared with our patients and other specialists through emails, WhatsApp or other means.

Intra-Oral X-Ray Sensor

The X-ray sensor enables the dentist to take oral X-ray and view it directly from the computer desktop or laptop. The sensor allows for rapid imagery with a lower radiation exposure and no need for the use of environmentally harmful chemicals for the development and fixing of the x- ray image. The availability of high tech diagnostic radiography also allows for time and cost efficiency and healthier imaging while our staffs high infection control awareness ensures a biologically safe environment in which cross infection is further prevented.

Endo-Motor Systems

The availability of the Endo-Motor Endodontic Machine allows the dentist to fully prepare and mechanically clean the affected root canal and completely obturate root canals during endodontic treatments. This improves treatment prognosis and ultimately provides value for money.

External Suction Unit

The availability of external suction unit enables continuous suction of saliva during oral procedures. It acts as a standby system incase of a failure of inbuilt suction system

Gutter Cutter

A modern machine used to cut root filling during endodontic treatment. Its tips are sterilizable and are smokeless.

Teeth Whitening Machine

A modern teeth whitening machine used for chemical teeth whitening.
